🤯 Did You Know?

Every SAIL on a boat is a triangle. Boat designers calculate triangle areas exactly — the wrong area means the boat won't catch enough wind and you drift in the ocean!

🔺 Triangle Area Formula
Area = (Base × Height) ÷ 2
A triangle is HALF a rectangle with the same base and height
