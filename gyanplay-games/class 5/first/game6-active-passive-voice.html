📖 Active vs Passive Voice
🔵 Active Voice
The dog chased the cat.
Subject: dog Verb: chased Object: cat
🟠 Passive Voice
The cat was chased by the dog.
New subject: cat Be + past participle By the dog
🔑 Passive Voice Formula
Object + be-verb + past participle + by + subject
be-verbs: is/are/was/were/has been/have been
Past participles: eaten, written, built, seen, loved
