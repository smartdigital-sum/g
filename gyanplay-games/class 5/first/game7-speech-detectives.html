🤯 Did You Know?

Everything in a newspaper is INDIRECT speech — "The minister said that the bridge would open." Without this rule, reporters would have to copy every word exactly! Reported speech IS journalism!

📚 DIRECT SPEECH → REPORTED SPEECH
👩‍🏫
Teacher
"I am teaching grammar today."
↓
She said that she was teaching grammar that day.
⏱️ Time Shift — Tense Changes
am/is/are→was/were
will→would
can→could
today→that day
I / my→he/she / his/her
