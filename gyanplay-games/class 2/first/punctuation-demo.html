How to Play

1. Read the sentence carefully

2. Choose the correct punctuation mark that should go at the end

3. Click on the punctuation button (. ? or !) to select your answer

4. Look for context clues:

  • Questions (who, what, where, why, how) usually need ?
  • Exclamations (surprise, excitement, strong feeling) need !
  • Statements (facts, descriptions) need .

Learning: This teaches proper punctuation usage in English sentences.
