How to Play

1. Look at the object shown (e.g., a rose, ice cream, bell)

2. Select which sense organ you would use to experience that object

3. Click on the object and then click on the sense organ

4. Click "Check Match" to see if you're correct

5. Learn about the five senses:

  • Eyes (Sight): For seeing colors, shapes, and light
  • Ears (Hearing): For hearing sounds and music
  • Nose (Smell): For smelling flowers, food, and scents
  • Tongue (Taste): For tasting sweet, sour, salty, bitter
  • Skin (Touch): For feeling textures, temperature, and pressure

Learning: This teaches children about the five senses and how we experience the world.
