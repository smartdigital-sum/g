Stage 2: Packaging Challenge
🍵 Assam Tea Tin
Diameter: 10cm, Height: 15cm
What is the minimum label area needed?
Correct! Curved Surface Area = 2πrh = 2 × 3.14 × 5 × 15 = 471 cm²
This is the label area. Total surface area would include the top and bottom circles too.

Stage 3: Cylinder Calculations
Curved SA = 2πrh = cm²
Total SA = 2πr(r+h) = cm²
Volume = πr²h = cm³
1 m³ = 1,000,000 cm³ (not 100!)

Stage 4: Unit Conversion Trap
⚠️ Common Mistake
Students often think: 1 m³ = 100 cm³ ❌
Convert 2.5 m³ to cm³:
Correct! 1 m = 100 cm, so 1 m³ = (100)³ cm³ = 1,000,000 cm³
Therefore, 2.5 m³ = 2.5 × 1,000,000 = 2,500,000 cm³
