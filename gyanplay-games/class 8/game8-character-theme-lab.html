J.K. Rowling was rejected by 12 publishers before Harry Potter was accepted. Each publisher who rejected her read the SAME words — but interpreted the character of Harry differently. Character is not written; it is interpreted. Great literature is a Rorschach test — you see what you bring to it.
Stage 1: Character Web
From "The Best Christmas Present in the World"
"The narrator finds an old letter describing how enemy soldiers came together on Christmas Day during World War I, sharing food and playing football, before returning to fight each other the next day."
Tap characters to see their traits:
Jim
